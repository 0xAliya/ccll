He helped the old man stand up.=他扶老人站了起来。
He picked up the books one by one.=他一本一本地把书捡起来。
Although he might be late, he decided to help him.=虽然他可能会迟到，但他还是决定帮助他。
He asked where the old man was going and learned that he was going to the bus stop.=他问老人要去哪里，得知老人也要去公交站。
So he walked with him in the rain.=于是他在雨中陪老人一起走。
He didn’t leave until the old man got on the bus.=直到老人上了公交车他才离开。
Later that evening, he was surprised to find a message on his phone.=那天晚上，他惊讶地发现手机上有一条消息。
It was from the old man.=那是老人发来的。
It said that the old man thanked him for his help.=内容是老人感谢他的帮助。
Though his clothes were wet, his heart was warm.=虽然衣服湿了，但他的心是暖的。
He smiled and realized that helping others made him happy.=他笑了，意识到帮助别人也会让自己快乐。
